What Is The WOW Cruise-In?

The WOW Cruise-In is a FREE Charity Car Cruise-In that attracts over 175 of Delmarva's Hottest Hotrods, Coolest Classic Cars & Trucks, 4WD Mud Bog Trucks & Custom Motorcycles which can be seen on display in Downtown Crisfield.

Who Is This Year's Charity Beneficiary?

Crisfield Arts & Entertainment District Project
